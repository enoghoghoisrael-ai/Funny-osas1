The Mind Behind the Brand

A proud alumnus of the University of Port Harcourt (UNIPORT), Israel successfully blends psychological intrigue with everyday Nigerian street humor.

Currently operating from Lekki, Lagos, his viral public setups and interactive content have established him as a household name with a digital footprint valued at over $50,000 USD.
